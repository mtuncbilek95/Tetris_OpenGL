glGenBuffers generates one or multiple vertex buffer objects.

Example:

unsigned int VBOS[5];
int LengthVBOS = sizeof(VBOS) / sizeof(VBOS[0]);

glGenBuffers(LengthVBOS, &VBOS);

This example shows that glGenBuffer will generate LengthVBOS amount of buffer 
objects which equals to 5 at the moment.
